A Little About Pinelands M.S

Our business.

Located in the outskirts of Brisbane, Pinelands Music School creates leaders in music performance and composition. Our class inspire the future generation of music professionals and are recognised my professional music boards across Australia. Our very experienced and dedicated teachers endeavor to help you learn a new instrument, or enhance your already acquired skills. Pinelands Music School has now created an online booking systems where you can view your upcoming lessions and teacher feedback. So what are you waiting for? Book now!
